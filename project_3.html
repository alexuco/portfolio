Case Study: Sail your dream

As a RYA skipper and diving instructor, a partner and I started a travel agency business, offering unrivaled luxury yacht charters

sailyourdream

  • Industry: Tourism
  • Size: 2+

Own project for myself and a partner, to jumping into the travel agency business.

Project Requirements

  • Implement a fully responsive solution
  • Improve overall aesthetic and user experience and make the site engaging and accessible
  • Fresh site with dynamic elements and specific content
  • Make an advertising spot and embed it

Project Overview

In this first stage I wanted something fresh and kind of minimalist at the same time in design. The idea was to have different sections clearly identified with each other. with the opportunity to build upon them as soon as possible.

The Challenge

Design everything from scratch is always a challenge in and of itself, however in this case, I made the prototypes in Figma and then jumped into the code editor. Another challenge was to make the advertising spot.... I spent the same amount of time to code as to make the commercial, but in the end I was very proud of it.

The Result

Landing page breathable, modern, responsive and understandable at a first glance.
